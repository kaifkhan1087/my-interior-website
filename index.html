Process

How we work with you

Clear steps so you always know what is happening with your project.

Every home and office is different. We start by listening to your needs — budget, style and timeline — and then build a plan that fits you instead of forcing a template.

Friendly, no-pressure discussion: You can first talk to us, share photos/videos of your space on WhatsApp and decide comfortably before starting any work.
1
Understand & Measure

We understand your requirement and, if needed, take basic measurements or review your floor plan.

2
Concept & Budget

We suggest layout options, design ideas and an approximate budget range as per your comfort.

3
Design & Material

Materials, colours and finishes are finalized; drawings or visual references are shared for clarity.

4
Execution Support

Work begins with regular updates. Final checks and finishing are done before handover.
